⚙️ Mini Gauge – Hyundai Coupé
  • Problem: Upgrading to an aftermarket 2DIN radio in Hyundai Coupé/Tiburon (GK FL 2002–2008) makes the original factory multi-gauge physically incompatible — it simply cannot fit.
  • Solution: A compact custom embedded device that displays battery voltage (hybrid ADC + CAN bus weighted average), coolant temperature from the Siemens SIMK43 ECU via CAN, and real-time fuel consumption (L/100 km while driving, L/h at idle).
  • Impact: Automatic night mode, 60 FPS UI via LVGL on a 1.28" round IPS LCD — seamlessly replacing the OEM gauge with no loss of information.
